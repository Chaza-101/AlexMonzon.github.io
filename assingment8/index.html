The reason I chose to make this is because My Chemical Romance, after so, so, so many years, are finally doing a stadium tour of their album Welcome to The Black Parade. They are coming to Chicago and while I wasn't able to get tickets (by the time I got to the checkout page the only tickets available were 300+) I still hold out hope I will get them before August comes. Devo are also the openers, I saw them last year, and they were spectacular and I cannot wait to see them again. While the song is called "The End.", this is the first song in the album. The lyrics are also the opening lyrics, and I think they set the tone for the rest of the album pretty well.
