At Curiosity, we are dedicated to creating an immersive and experimental nightlife experience that sparks the imagination and ignites the senses. Our underground haven invites you to step into a world of unexpected themes, from the mysterious to the fantastical, and everything in between. Each event is crafted to transport our guests into an entirely new realm where curiosity reigns and boundaries are pushed. We pride ourselves on being a space where creativity, self-expression, and the art of discovery collide-offering a sanctuary for those who dare to explore the unknown. Join us at Curiosity and embrace the strange, the wild, and the wonderfully weird.

Curiosity was founded by a dynamic duo of visionaries-Jasper Nightingale and Lena Ashford; whose shared love for unconventional art, immersive experiences, and experimental nightlife led them to create a one-of-a-kind venue in the heart of Chicago. With backgrounds in event production, design, and underground culture, they envisioned a space that would push boundaries and challenge the ordinary.
